Your Job: Verify, Don't Trust

LLMs sound confident while being wrong. Your goal is to apply a critical mindset. A successful outcome is knowing what you cannot verify.

Use this verification spectrum to log your work:

  • ‚úì Verified: You proved it yourself.
  • ‚úó Error: You found a flaw.
  • ? Uncertain: You'd need to ask an expert.
  • ‚ö† Trusted: You had to take it on faith.

Your verification log is the most valuable output of this workshop.
